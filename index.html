WEDDING DETAILS

Ceremony

The ceremony will begin at 3:00pm at the Kentlands Mansion in Gaithersburg, Maryland. Light refreshments will follow shortly after. Ceremony will be outdoors (weather permitting). Dress is semi-formal.

Kentlands Mansion is located about 40 minutes north of Washington, DC. Please allow between 45 and 60 minutes travel time if leaving from Washington, DC. Ample parking will be available on-site.

Kentlands Mansion
320 Kent Square Rd
Gaithersburg, MD 20878

Reception

The reception will begin at 6:00PM at Far East Restaurant in Rockville, Maryland, about 20 minutes south of Kentlands Mansion. Start heading over around 5:30. Please allow 25 minutes of travel time. There will be plenty of on-site parking will be available.

Beer and wine will be served at the reception by a licensed bartender. If you are 21 or older and wish to partake, please make sure to bring your ID.

Far East Restaurant
5055 Nicholson Lane
Rockville, MD 20852

Travel and Accommodations

If you are joining us from out of town, you may wish to book accommodations. We do not have a room block available, but feel free to search for hotels in Gaithersburg, Rockville, or Silver Spring, Maryland. Many other lodging accommodations are available closer to Washington, DC and Baltimore; we recommend you look for accommodations in those areas there if you're planning on exploring either city over the weekend. If a hotel room is prohibitedly expensive, or otherwise unavailable, we highly recommend AirBnB as a cost-effective alternative.

We also recommend flying into Baltimore-Washington International Airport (BWI). Dulles International Airport (IAD) and Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport (DCA) are nearby options as well, but further out.
